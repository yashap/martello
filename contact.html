Contact and Registration

We hope that you are interested in Martello for your child. If so, please contact the school by filling out the form below, or call and speak with our Vice Principal, Anne-Lorraine Molloy. We’ll make an appointment for you and your family to meet the administrative staff and teachers and to view the school facilities. Next we’ll schedule a visit for your child to spend a part or full morning or a day in a specific class on their own to see how they enjoy a Martello day. Once you have decided on Martello for your child, and we certainly hope you do, you will receive a registration package with forms, tuition and after school program information to get you started.
